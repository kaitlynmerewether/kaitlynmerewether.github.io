SCA Events are produced by SCA Ltd

SCA, founded in 2001 is a UK based independent life science Events Company with predominantly business and academic client base. The key strategic objective of SCA is to communicate science and medical research between academia, clinical practice and the pharmaceutical industry. Most of its events are in Australia and Perth or live streamed. SCA expanded its operations to international in association with Meetings International, Singapore. All major meetings of SCA and Meetings International are accredited with Continued Professional Education (CPD), Continued Education (CE), Continued Medical Education (CME) Credits.
